I am a high school student who loves to travel the world

Join me as I travel around the world.

by Cathy Ma

Summer in the Winter

All around, the northern parts of the United States were cold, but I was basking in the sun. Winter of 2019, my family and I decided to take a trip to Key West to celebrate Christmas. Throughout a whole year, we rarely go out on vacation because of how busy it is, however, Christmas is a time when my parents decided a break is well deserved.

Our vacation started out with a 14 hour trip down to Key West that took 2 days to achieve. After driving 7 hours during the first day, we stopped at Daytona Beach to rest for the night. While in Daytona, there was a festival going on, so we decided to check it out. It wasn't much, but people there went wild. After staying at the festival for about a hour, everyone became extremely tired and turned in for the night.

The next morning, I awoke to my mom pulling me up and saying we're going to watch the sunrise on the beach behind the hotel. Reluctantly I got up and took some pictures in the cool breezy morning.

We then hit the road again, 7 hours later, we make it to Key West and check in to the hotel. The next day, my parents woke my sibling and I up early to explore. We quickly got ready, ate breakfast and reserved a seat on the bus that leaves at 9am to the center of Key West. From there, we start to wander around aimlessly, looking for activities. Both my sibling and I didn't care for any of the activities, so we settled for a coral reef viewing on a glass-bottom boat. After that, we continued to wander and mess around on the surrounding beaches. The next day, my mom wanted to visit the southern most point of the Continental US, so we made our way to the attraction. By the time we got to the location, there were a lot of people in line to take a picture with the structure symbolizing the southernmost point.

After taking photos as every tourist does, my family and I started to aimlessly wander again. All of us like to enjoy the nature of the area and the attractions that can be seen without tickets, which is why we like to wander around a lot when vacationing. Another day passes by and it is time for us to pack our bags and make our way back to North Carolina. My parents have work and my sibling and I had school, thus our time in Key West started to come to a end.

On the way back up from Key West, my parents implemented the same system used when going to Key West. Drive half way in the first day and finish the drive in the second day. Similarly to the drive down, we stop at Daytona Beach for the night. The next day, we rise quite early and hit the road. Along the way, my parents stopped at Hard Rock Cafe Hotel to do some business while my sibling and I wandered around the hotel. Afterwards, I found a museum I wanted to visit, so we set off to the museum. After spending 3 to 4 hours in the museum, we get back on the highway, driving the whole way home without anymore distractions.
